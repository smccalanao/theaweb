Blood is Thicker Than Water

by Althea Suzanne E. Colindres

A celebration is a normal celebration, but with family, it became something special. Every occasion should be celebrated, even the simple ones. It can be birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, or even fishing. Every achievement in our life should be celebrated with our loved ones, our family. Our family's tradition is something that I'm proud of. I was always looking forward to celebrating with them. Every time someone achieves something within our family, we don't forget to celebrate it. We celebrated because we treasure them and we are proud of them. For instance, like my 7-year-old cousin, he was a boy, and he won the chess game last week, and we celebrated because that achievement is something you're proud of. Or like one time we went fishing with our whole family, and suddenly my 7-year-old cousin boy, or we called him Ak Boy, caught a big fish, and just like that we celebrated as we grilled the fish he caught.

However, there is no perfect tradition and family. Just like ours, there would be a flaw that makes our tradition imperfect. We're not always complete during this celebration. They would give their donation for the celebration, but they are far away from us. And sometimes, that's not the only reason. Our family sometimes fought with each other, which would lead to hatred. And because of this, our celebration becomes sorrowful. Just like when we celebrated the anniversary of my auntie J, we celebrated it in my other Tito's house; my father said he would barge in, drunk, and start hurling hurtful words towards my favourite cousin. And because of this incident, the result was a disaster because of this. My Auntie J went home, and my father fought with my other Tita. Celebration is always special when celebrated with our family. I will achieve becoming more special when we celebrate with our loved ones. I just realised that our tradition has its own uniqueness and flaws. There's no such thing as aect one; just like our tradition, family was never once perfect, ever. We fought sometimes, but we always found each other to forgive those who wronged us. It may not be simple because forgiveness is not easy to give. But let me remind you that there is giving and forgiveness, so we should give forgiveness because we deserve it. Celebrate our achievement with our family because blood is thicker than water.

Evaluating an Advertisement: "Stop the violence. Don’t drink, don’t drive."

by Althea Suzanne E. Colindres

Source: I found it on Behance, which is where creative people share their work. It tells me this ad was made by talented people who know how to get a message across.

Context: This image shows the danger of being drunk and still pursue to drive. Because of this, many accidents happened and some people died during or after these accidents.

Contents: This creative picture shows a being punched in the face, however, if we look closer, there’s an image drawn in the man face and in the hand. The drawings show the car crushing together symbolizing the accidents that might happen when we are drunk and still pursuing to drive. Under to the car crushing, there’s these words saying “Stop the violence. Don’t drunk and drive.” A very creative way to advertise about the violence that might happen when we are driving while drunk.

Audience: This advertisement is for everyone, especially to those people who love to drink alcohol and continue to drive while being drunk.

Image Creator: It was created by Terremoto Propaganda, an advertising agency from Brazil. They seem to make ads that are really creative and get people’s attention.

Visual: The visual of this image is attractive that would make you to stop walking and stare it for longer time. That’s how powerful the image was made.

Technical Quality: The photo is high quality and well-done. The way they painted the cars on the man’s face and hand is really clever, and the text is easy to read. The advertisement is very well-made and professional.
